Pasta with Red Sauce

Description:

Pasta with red sauce is a super simple recipe and a quick snack to make when you are in a work rush. I hope you enjoy the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Red Sauce from store bought jar (1 serving)
  • Pasta, any kind (1 serving)

Directions:

  1. Start boiling water and add a pinch of salt
  2. Add pasta to the water and let boil for 10 minutes
  3. Remove pasta, strain, and add to a bowl or plate
  4. Add pasta sauce to bowl/plate. Mix and enjoy!
