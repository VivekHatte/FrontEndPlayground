Veggie Sandwich

Description:

This is a simple veggie sandwhich to make when you are hungry for something to eat fast for lunch. Give it a go, especially with double cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (your favorite)
  • Your choice of veggies
  • 2 slices of your favorite cheese
  • hot sauce or your favorite sauces

Directions:

  1. Toast both slices of bread
  2. Add slices of cheese on the toast in a plate
  3. Add veggies to the sandwhich
  4. Add sauces to the sandwhich
  5. Enjoy!
